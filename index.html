About Me

I'd rather make apps than sell them

My personality type is INTP. Apparently that makes me the analytical type? Rational. Introverted.
Whatever you want to call it.

I always liked technology, but until recently I was focused on selling it. As a generalist at a software reseller, I had a surface-level relationship with many brands like Microsoft, Cisco, HPE, and Proofpoint, but wanted to learn what happens behind the scenes. I was fascinated by the Dev teams at both the startups I briefly worked with. They owned their craft and knew their products inside and out. I knew just enough to sound credible on the phone to customer. Always curious about the other side, I decided to to "take a look under the hood" by taking ElevenFifty's Web Dev course and I'm glad I did.
